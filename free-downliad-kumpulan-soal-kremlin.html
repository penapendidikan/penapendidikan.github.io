EXERCISE 1: Identify the tenses of the following sentences.

Tugas 1. Identifikasi tense dari kalimat - kalimat berikut.

1.  Doctor Sowanso

Doctor Sowanso is the Secretary General of the United  Nations. He's one of the busiest men in the world. He's  just landed at New Delhi Airport now. The Indian Prime  Minister is meeting him. Later, they'll talk about Asian

problems.  Yesterday he was in Moscow. He visited the Kremlin and  had lunch with Soviet leaders. During lunch, they  discussed international politics.  Tomorrow he'll fly to Nairobi. He'll meet the President  of Kenya and other African leaders. He'll be there for 12  hours.  The day after tomorrow, he'll be in London. He'll meet  the British Minister and they'll talk about European  Economic problems,

Terjemahan:

Dokter Sowanso adalah sekretaris jenderal dari PBB. Beliau adalah salah satu orang tersibuk di dunia. Dia baru saja tiba di bandara New Dehlhi sekarang. Perdana menteri India sedang menemuinya. Kemudian mereka akan membicarakan tentang masalah Asia. Kemarin, beliau di Moskow. Beliau mengunjungi Kremlin dan makan siang dengan pemipin soviet. Selama makan siang, mereka mendiskusikan politik internasional. Besok beliau akan terbang ke Nairobi. Beliau akan bertemu presiden Kenya dan pemimpin Afrika lainnya. Beliau akan bertemu menteri Inggris dan mereka akan berbicara tentang masalah ekonomi Eropa.

Pembahasan

Doctor Sowanso is the Secretary General of the United  Nations = Simple present tense

He's one of the busiest men in the world. = Simple present tense

He's  just landed at New Delhi Airport now. = Present perfect tense

The Indian Prime  Minister is meeting him.  = Present continuous tense

Later, they'll talk about Asian  problems= Simple future tense

Yesterday he was in Moscow. = Simple past tense

He visited the Kremlin and  had lunch with Soviet leaders. = Simple past tense

During lunch, they  discussed international politics.  = Simple past tense

Tomorrow he'll fly to Nairobi. = Simple future tense

He'll meet the President  of Kenya and other African leaders. = Simple future tense

He'll be there for 12  hours.  = Simple future tense

The day after tomorrow, he'll be in London. = Simple future tense

He'll meet  the British Minister and they'll talk about European  Economic problems,  = Simple future tense

Macam - macam tenses dan contohnya:

Simple present tense: Menceritakan kejadian yang berlangsung sekarang atau terjadi berulang-ulang (kebiasaan)

Subject + Verb 1 + Object

Doctor Sowanso (S) is (V) the Secretary General of the United  Nations (O)

Simple future tense:

Subject + will/be going to + Verb dasar + Object

He (S) will be ( Verb) there (Adverb) for 12  hours

Simple past tense:

Subject + Verb 2 + Object

He (S) visited (V)  the Kremlin (O)

Present perfect tense:

Subject + has/have + Verb 3 + Object

He (S) has just landed (V) at New Delhi Airport now (ADV)

Present continuous tense:  

Subject + Verb dasar -ing + Object

The Indian Prime  Minister (S) is meeting (V) him (O)

Doctor Sowanso (S) is (V) the Secretary General of the United  Nations (O)

The Indian Prime  Minister (S) is meeting (V) him (O)
