18. apa perbedaan sign dengan symbol


Both signs and symbols are graphical presentations but people often use them interchangeably. The maindifference between the two is that asign is a language on its own and it is used to communicate something to people while a symbol is subjective in nature and often requires interpretation

19. Pengertian sign and symbol dalam bahasa inggris dong...


Symbol: a mark, sign or word that is understood as representing and idea, object, or relationship.

Sign: an object, quality, or event whose presence/occurence indicates the probable presence/occurence of something else.
