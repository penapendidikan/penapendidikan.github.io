Opinion (Pendapat):

1. "In my opinion, the new policy on flexible work hours is a great initiative for improving work-life balance."

2. "From my perspective, the movie adaptation didn't do justice to the depth of the characters in the book."

Thoughts (Pemikiran):

1. "I've been thinking a lot about climate change lately, and it's clear that urgent action is needed to address this global issue."

2. "Lately, I've been reflecting on the importance of effective communication in building strong relationships, both personally and professionally."

Responding to Opinion (Menanggapi Pendapat):

1. "I completely agree with you. Having the option to adjust our work hours can definitely help reduce stress and increase productivity."

2. "I understand what you mean. Sometimes, film adaptations struggle to capture the nuances that the original book presents."

Responding to Thoughts (Menanggapi Pemikiran):

1. "I share your concern about climate change. It's crucial that governments, organizations, and individuals work together to find sustainable solutions."

2. "Your thoughts on communication resonate with me. It's amazing how much better we can connect with others when we truly listen and express ourselves clearly."

CONVERSATION OF AGREEMENT: EXPRESSING DISAGREEMENT

Pembaca, tahukah anda masing-masing dari kita pasti akan dihadapkan pada situasi ketika harus menyatakan pendapat. Setiap pikiran yang ada dalam kepala manusia tentu tidaklah ada yang sama persis. Ambil contoh saja orang kembar, mereka tidak selamanya selalu setuju loh tentang satu hal. Ada kalanya kita berseberangan dengan pendapat orang lain.

Lalu apa yang biasanya kita lakukan jika pikiran kita atau pendapat kita berbeda dengan orang lain? Nah jawabannya ya tentu sampaikan, apalagi jika kapasitas anda berada dalam sebuah diskusi untuk mengambil keputusan. Pendapat 1 orang pun akan memberikan masukan yang bermanfaat.

Dalam bahasa inggris sendiri kita menganal istilah untuk menyampaikan pendapat tersebut dalam bentuk expressing of agreement and disagreement. Pada artikel kali ini kita akan lebih banyak membahas tentang ekspresi tidak setuju (expressing disagreement). Kadangkala kita merasa sungkan untuk menolak, atau menyatakan ketidaksetujuan akan suatu hal. Namun anda harus berani menyuarakan hal tersebut, terlebih pada kesempatan-kesempatan tertentu.

In English, opinion and thoughts are rather different in their definition. Opinion is basically a statement about what you have in mind about something, either agree or disagree, pro or con and so on. Thoughts are actually ideas or what you think about something. In my opinion, ...
