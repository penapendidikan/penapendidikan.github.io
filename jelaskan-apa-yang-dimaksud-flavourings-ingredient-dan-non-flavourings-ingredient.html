9. cari ingredient ama cara pembuatan nya


Ingredients (Bahan):

- a can of chocolate milk  (1 kaleng susu coklat)

- three packs of milk biscuits  (3 bungkus biskuit susu)

- one pack of colorful chocolate sprinkles  (1 bungkus meses coklat warna-warni)

- toothpicks  (tusuk gigi)

- small cookie paper (kertas roti kecil)

Steps (Langkah pembuatan) :

1. Smash the biscuit until it seems like a powder.  (Hancurkan biskuit sampai berbentuk bubuk)

2. Stir.  (Aduk)

3. Add the chocolate milk,  while stirring throroughly until the mixture becomes hard.  Make sure it's not too runny . (Tambahkan susu coklat sambil diaduk sampai adonan mengeras. Pastikan tidak terlalu encer)

4. Form the mixture into balls . (Bentuk adonan menjadi bola)

5. Roll the ball in colorful chocolate sprinkles  (Gulingkan bola di meses cokelat warna-warni)

6. Place in small cookie paper  (Letakkan di kertas roti)

7. Stick toothpicks for handles (Tusukkan tusuk gigi sebagai pegangan)

34. what is the active ingredient of the drug?​


An active ingredient is the ingredient in a pharmaceutical drug or pesticide that is biologically active. The similar terms active pharmaceutical ingredient (also abbreviated as API) and bulk active are also used in medicine, and the term active substance may be used for natural products.

Jawaban:

An active ingredient is an ingredient in a pharmaceutical drug that is biologically active.
