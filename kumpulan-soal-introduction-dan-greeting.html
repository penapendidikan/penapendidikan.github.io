10. buatlah percakapan percakapan singkat yang berisi “greeting, self introduction, personal information, dan state of being / feeling!


Tentu, berikut ini adalah percakapan singkat yang mencakup greeting, self introduction, personal information, dan state of being/feeling:

A: Good morning!

B: Good morning! How are you today?

A: I'm doing well, thanks. My name is John. I'm from New York, and I work as a software engineer. How about you?

B: Nice to meet you, John. I'm Sarah. I'm originally from California, and I'm a student majoring in biology.

A: That sounds interesting, Sarah. How do you feel about your studies?

B: Well, I find it challenging but fascinating. I'm really passionate about biology, so I'm enjoying it.

A: That's great to hear. I'm also quite passionate about my work in software engineering. It's a field I love, and I feel content with what I do.

B: It's wonderful to do what you love and feel content about it. That's how it should be in life.

A: Absolutely, Sarah. So, what do you like to do in your free time?

B: In my free time, I enjoy reading, hiking, and spending time with my friends.

A: Those are some wonderful hobbies. I like to go cycling and explore new places when I have free time.

B: Cycling is a great way to stay active and explore. It sounds like we both enjoy being outdoors.

A: Yes, indeed. It was nice talking to you, Sarah.

B: Likewise, John. Have a great day!
