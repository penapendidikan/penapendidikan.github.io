a. Persamaan umum barisan bilangan tersebut adalah [tex]\bf {\frac{1}{2}.2}^{n-1}[/tex].

b. Suku ke-7 barisan tersebut adalah 32.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Barisan geometri adalah barisan yang memiliki pembanding atau yang biasa disebut rasio. Rasio ini bisa berupa hasil bagi ataupun hasil kali bilangan yang sama dengan suku sebelumnya.

[tex] \: [/tex]

Rumus suku ke-n

[tex]{a.r}^{n-1}[/tex]

[tex] \: [/tex]

Keterangan

Un → Suku ke-n

a → Suku pertama (U1)

r → Rasio

[tex] \: [/tex]

Rumus mencari rasio

r = [tex]\frac{{U}_{n}}{{U}_{n-1}}[/tex]

Jika dijabarkan maka menjadi :

r = [tex]\frac{{U}_{2}}{{U}_{1}}[/tex]

r = [tex]\frac{{U}_{3}}{{U}_{2}}[/tex]

r = [tex]\frac{{U}_{4}}{{U}_{3}}[/tex]

Dan seterusnya.

[tex] \: [/tex]

Penyelesaian soal

Diketahui :

a = [tex]\frac{1}{2}[/tex]

r = [tex]\frac{1}{\frac{1}{2}}[/tex] = 2

[tex] \: [/tex]

Ditanya :

a. Persamaan umum barisan bilangan tersebut.

b. Suku ke-7.

[tex] \: [/tex]

Jawab :

a. Persamaan umum

Un = [tex]{a.r}^{n-1}[/tex]

= [tex]{\frac{1}{2}.2}^{n-1}[/tex]

[tex] \: [/tex]

b. Suku ke-7

[tex]{\frac{1}{2}.2}^{n-1}[/tex]

= [tex]{\frac{1}{2}.2}^{7-1}[/tex]

= [tex]{\frac{1}{2}.2}^{6}[/tex]

= 2⁵

= 32

[tex] \: [/tex]

Kesimpulan

a. Persamaan umum barisan bilangan tersebut adalah [tex]{\frac{1}{2}.2}^{n-1}[/tex].

b. Suku ke-7 barisan tersebut adalah 32.

35. Tolong Bantu Soal Kls 7 Semester 2 bab 2 task 1 ​


SIMPLE PRESENT

1.

(+) Yasmina cooks a new recipe every Sunday morning.

(-) Yasmina doesn't cook a new recipe every Sunday morning.

(?) Does Yasmina cook a new recipe every Sunday morning?

2.

(+) My father and I paint the wall once a year.

(-) My father and I don't paint the wall once a year.

(?) Do My father and I paint the wall once a year?

3.

(+) Tamara finishes her lesson at school at 2 p.m. every day.

(-) Tamara doesn't finish her lesson at school at 2 p.m. every day.

(?) Does Tamara finish her lesson at school at 2 p.m. every day?

4.

(+) Harya has a guitar course every Wednesday.

(-) Harya doesn't have a guitar course every Wednesday.

(?) Does Harya have a guitar course every Wednesday.

5.

(+) The students have English subject every Monday.

(-) The students don't have English subject every Monday.

(?) Do the students have English subject every Monday?

EXPLANATION

Simple present ➡ menjelaskan kebiasaan, kegiatan berulang, kebenaran umum, fakta dan kegiatan yang sudah terjadwalkan dalam waktu dekat.

Penanda kata:

Always (selalu)Often (sering)Sometimes (kadang²)Seldom (jarang)Everyday (setiap hari) Every Monday (setiap Senin)Once a year (setahun sekali)

Pola:

➡ Verbal

(+) S+verb¹(s/es)+O/C

(-) S+do/does+not+verb¹+O/C

(?) Do/does+S+verb¹+O/C ?

Contoh:

(+) Yasmina cooks a new recipe every Sunday morning.

(-) Yasmina doesn't cook a new recipe every Sunday morning.

(?) Does Yasmina cook a new recipe every Sunday morning?

Jika subjeknya (I, they, we, you dan kata benda jamak), pakailah "Do" pada kalimat negatifnya dan kalimat tanyannya. Kata kerja tidak ditambahkan akhiran -s/es jika subjeknya jamak pada kalimat positifnya.Jika subjeknya (she, he, it), gunakanlah "Does" pada kalimat negatifnya dan tanyannya.
