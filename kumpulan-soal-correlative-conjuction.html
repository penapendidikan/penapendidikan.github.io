2. contoh teks tentang correlative conjuction


This research is purposed to analyze the correlation between students’ vocabulary mastery and their reading ability. In this research, the writer used quantitative method by Pearson correlation product moment. There are two variables in this research. Students’ vocabulary as independent variable (variable X) and Reading ability as dependent variable (variable Y), the technique of collecting data was by using a test. The test consists of 20 items of vocabulary test and 20 items of reading comprehension. The object of study is 40 students from class VIII A at the second grade students of SMP YMJ Ciputat in the academic year 2010/2011. From the calculation by using product moment formula, it was found out that the mean of the scores of the vocabulary test is 60.375. The mean of the scores of the students’ achievement in reading comprehension is 45.75. The result of applying the rxy (ro) distribution shows that the coefficient correlation is 0.68. The degree of significance 5% is 0.304 and the degree of significance 1% is 0.393. It means ro is higher than rt (0.68 > 0.304) and (0.68 > 0.393). So, alternative hypothesis (Ha) is accepted and null hypothesis (Ho) is rejected. The conclusion of this research is there is a significant correlation between students’ vocabulary mastery and their reading ability.

oordinating conjunctions adalah konjungsi yang digunakan untuk menghubungkan kata, frase, atau klausa (independent clause) yang mempunyai kedudukan setara atau struktur gramatikal yang sama dalam kalimat. Ada tujuh konjungsi yang digunakan: for, and, nor, but, or, yet, so (biasa disingkat FANBOYS untuk memudahkan dalam mengingat).


Perhatikan contoh penggunaan coordinating conjunctions yang menghubungkan kata dengan kata, frase dengan frase, dan klausa dengan klausa (yang diberi huruf tebal adalah konjungsi, sedangkan yang bergaris bawah adalah kata, frase, atau klausa).

a. Kata dengan kata (subjek, predikat/verba, objek)
- You and I will come together.
- It can be black, white or grey.
- Most children like cookies and milk.
- Lilacs and violets are usually purple.

b. Frase dengan frase
- The gold is hidden at the beach or by the lakeside.
- Daniel's uncle claimed that he spent most of his youth dancing on rooftops and swallowing goldfish.
- Manufacturers must be free to develop their ideas and to package their products. 
- He reappeared a few minutes later looking embarrassed yet appearing otherwise unruffled. 

c. Klausa dengan klausa
- I wanted to sit in the front of the balcony, so I ordered my tickets early.
- It’s an old car, but it’s very reliable.
- It was raining, so I took my umbrella.
- The sun is warm, yet the air is cool.
- I don’t care for the beach, but I enjoy a good vacation in the mountains.

27. pattern of correlative conjuction not only ... but also...


║CORRELATIVE CONJUNCTION-PAIRED CONJUNCTION║

Answer

Secara garis besar penulisan Correlative Conjunction, bentuk noun ( kata benda) , adjective ( kata sifat ), verb ( kata kerja ), harus memiliki pasangan yang  sama dalam kalimat.

Gambaran pola correlative conjunction

Both + noun + and + noun

Not only + verb + but also + verb

Either + noun + or + noun

Neither + adjective + nor + adjective

Example

=> "Not only + noun+but also + noun"

Not only my mother but also my sister came to my grandmother's house.

=> "Not only + adjective + but also + adjective

Dee is not only pretty, but also diligent.

=> "Not only +verb+but also+verb

Dee not only reads the magazine but also writes a letter.

Explanation

PAIRED CONJUNCTIONS( CORRELATIVE CONJUNCTIONS)

Merupakan kata penghubung yang berpasangan dan diposisikan berdasarkan konteks kalimat.

4 KATEGORI PAIRED CONJUNCTIONS :

1. EITHER ...OR.

Kata penghubung berpasangan  ini digunakan untuk menggabungkan ungkapan  positif yang merujuk pada sebuah pilihan.

Example  :

I can choose either English or Physic. { Aku dapat memilih baik bahasa Inggris ataupun Fisika }

=>Kalimat tersebut menyatakan tidak ada permasalahan jika saya memilih dari kedua mata pelajaran tersebut.

2. NEITHER....NOR.

Kata penghubung berpasangan ini digunakan untuk ungkapan negatif.

Example  :

Neither do I nor my sister likes the junk foods.

=>Kalimat tersebut menyatakan bahwa saya dan adik saya sama sama tidak menyukai makanan junk  ( makanan yang tidak mengandung banyak nutrisi )

3.  NOT ONLY......BUT ALSO

Kata penghubung berpasangan ini digunakan untuk pernyataan kalimat penekanan yang artinya "kebersamaan"

Example  :

Not only my mother but also my sisters accompany me to the hospital.

=>Kalimat tersebut menyatakan "kebersamaan"( bersama-sama) dimana adik saya dan ibu saya menemani saya ke rumah sakit.

4. BOTH.....AND

Kata penghubung berpasangan ini menyatakan adanya penekanan informasi.

Example :

This film is appropriate for both children and for adults.

=> Kalimat tersebut ada penekanan informasi bahwa film nya sesuai utk semua umur dan tidak ada pengecualian.

Struktur penulisan CORRELATIVE conjunction

Jika menggunakan "Both...and" , subjek -verb agreement nya bentuk jamak karena memiliki sifat penekanan kekuatan.

Jika menggunakan either...or, neither ...nor, not only ....but also menggunakan Subject-verb agreement nya disesuaikan dengan subyek terdekat nya.

Example  :

Neither I nor my FRIENDS are here.

Neither I nor my SISTER is here

=> Harap cermati penulisan kata penghubung berpasangan "neither...nor " terdapat perbedaan pada kalimat pertama dengan yang kedua. Penggunaan tobe jamak "are" pada kalimat pertama menyesuaikan subject terdekatnya "my friends." Sedangkan kalimat kedua , penggunaan tobe tunggal "is" karena menyesuaikan subject terdekatnya "my sister."

Untuk menjawab soal berikut ini kita harus menggunakan kalimat paralel dengan correlative conjunctions. Berikut ini adalah penggunaan setiap paralel strukturnya:

Both…and

Jake is going to the market. Lena is going to the market.Both Jake and Lena are going to the market

Either…or

Maybe Jake will go to the market. Maybe Lena will go to the market.Either Jake or Lena will go to the market.

Not only…but also

Jake is going to the market. Lena is going to the market.Not only Jake but also Lena are going to the market.

Neither…nor

Jake is not going to the market. Lena is not going to the market.Neither Jake nor Lena is going to the market.
Pembahasan

1. Tika is studying English. Tika is studying math.

Answer: Tika is not only studying English but also Math.


2. Indonesia exports oil to Japan. Indonesia exports iron to Japan.

Answer: Indonesia is not only exports oil but also iron to Japan.


3. Radio can spread the news fast. Television can spread the news fast.

Answer: Not only radio, but also television can spread the news fast.


4. The team last their way in the jungle. The team suffered from disease in the jungle.

Answer: The team not only last their way but also suffered from disease in the jungle.


5. Miss Yanti has met my father. Miss Yanti has met my mother.

Answer: Miss Yanti has met both my father and my mother.


6. Basuki is interested in reading a magazine. I am interested in reading magazine.

Answer: Both Basuki and I are interested in reading a magazine.


7. The girls like singing. The girls like dancing.

Answer: The girls like both singing and dancing.


8. We have visited Bali. We have visited Toba lake.

Answer: We have visited both Bali and Toba Lake.

Correlative Conjunction

1. The presentation was interesting. It was also informative.

➥ The presentation was not only interesting but also informative.

2. I was disappointed to hear that they don't offer a photography club. They also don't have a yearbook club.

➥ I was disappointed to hear that they neither offer a photography club nor have a yearbook club.

3. However, we can take art as an elective. Or we can take music.

➥ However, we can take either art or music as an elective.

4. Charlotte loves films. Her brother loves sports.

➥ Just as Charlotte loves films, so does her brother love sports.

5. Christine is a bookworm. So is Patrick.

➥ Both Christine and Patrick are bookworms.

6. The coach noticed that the entire team came to the car wash, not just one player.

➥ The coach noticed that not just one player, but the entire team came to the car wash.

7. Richard asked if Chloe would like to go shopping. He also wondered if she wanted to go to the beach.  

➥ Richard asked whether Chloe would rather go shopping or go to the beach.

-------------------

Preface

Correlative conjunction atau paired conjunction adalah sepasang konjungsi yang selalu digunakan bersamaan. Correlative conjunction digunakan untuk menghubungkan dua kata/frasa/independent clause yang kedudukan gramatikalnya setara (misalnya noun dan noun).

Beberapa correlative conjunction yang umum digunakan adalah:

both ... and ...not only ... but also ...either ... or ...neither ... nor ...just as ... so ...not ... but ...whether ... or ...

---------------

Explication

1. Not only ... but also ...  

Correlative conjunction ini digunakan untuk menggabungkan dua item dan menekankan item terakhir yang biasanya berlawanan dengan pengetahuan atau harapan pembicara.

Contoh:

The girl is not only pretty but also smart. (Pembicara terkejut bahwa gadis cantik juga pintar).

2.  Neither ... nor ...

Correlative conjuction ini digunakan untuk menyatakan bahwa dua pilihan/item tidak tersedia.

Contoh:

I have neither a car nor a motorcycle. (Pembicara tidak mempunyai keduanya).

3. Either ... or ...

Kita menggunakan either ... or ... ketika kita memberikan dua pilihan dan hanya salah satu dari kedua item tersebut yang bisa dipilih.

Contoh:

The students can use pens with either black or blue ink. They can't use pens with green ink.

4. Just as ... so ...

Correlative conjunction ini digunakan untuk menggabungkan dua independent clause yang menyatakan hal yang mirip.

Contoh:

Just as Indonesians eat rice, so do Germans eat potatoes.

Biasanya klausa sesudah so mengalami inversi (auxiliary verbnya diletakkan sebelum subjek) tapi jika informal, boleh tidak inversi.

Just as Indonesians eat rice, so Germans eat potatoes.

5. Both ... and ...

Kita menggunakan both ... and ... untuk menekankan bahwa dua item dalam kalimat itu benar adanya. Fungsi both ... and ... sebenarnya sama dengan and, bedanya dalam penekanan.

Bandingkan:

He is tall and handsome.

He is both tall and handsome.

Jika both ... and ... digunakan dalam subjek, subjek selalu menjadi plural, maka kata kerjanya juga plural. Pada subjek dengan correlative conjunction lainnya kata kerja mengikuti subjek terakhir.

Contoh:

Both Amy and Suzie are highschool students.

Either Amy or Suzie is a highschool student.

6. Not ... but ...

Correlative conjunction ini digunakan ketika kita ingin menyatakan dua hal yang bertentangan. Item pertama dalam negatif dan penekanan pada item kedua.

Contoh:

I was not surprised but excited to hear the news.

7. If/whether ... or ...

Kita menggunakan whether ... or ... untuk menyatakan keraguan atau mempertanyakan antara dua pilihan.

Contoh:

I don't know to wear whether the red dress or the black dress for the party.
