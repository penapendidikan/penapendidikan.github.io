6. Usp Kimia KODE SOAL : 8eei


Diketahui :

Asam Cuka (CH3COOH) 0,2 M

Ka=1×10^-5

Ditanya : pH=...?

[H+]^2=Ka.M

[H+]^2=1×10^-5.0,2

[H+]^2=2×10^-6

[H+]=10^-3akar 2

pH= -log[H+]

pH= -log10^-3.akar2

pH= 3-logakar2

Jadi pH nya yaitu 3-logakar2.
