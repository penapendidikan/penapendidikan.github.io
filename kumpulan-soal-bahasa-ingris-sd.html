6. kumpulan kata serapan ingris ke dalam bahasa indonesia​


Jawaban:

Ada banyak kata serapan dari bahasa Inggris yang telah masuk ke dalam bahasa Indonesia. Berikut adalah beberapa contoh kata serapan dari bahasa Inggris:

1. **Komputer (Computer)**: Kata ini digunakan untuk merujuk kepada perangkat elektronik yang digunakan untuk pemrosesan data.

2. **Televisi (Television)**: Merupakan alat untuk menampilkan gambar dan suara dari jarak jauh.

3. **Internet (Internet)**: Mengacu pada jaringan global yang menghubungkan berbagai perangkat komputer di seluruh dunia.

4. **Email (Electronic Mail)**: Merupakan pesan elektronik yang dikirim melalui internet.

5. **Handphone (Handphone)**: Dalam bahasa Inggris dikenal sebagai "mobile phone" atau "cell phone."

6. **Restoran (Restaurant)**: Tempat makan atau kedai yang menyajikan makanan dan minuman.

7. **Film (Film)**: Merujuk kepada produksi audiovisual yang ditayangkan di bioskop atau televisi.

8. **Majalah (Magazine)**: Publikasi cetak atau digital yang berisi artikel, gambar, dan iklan.

9. **Musik (Music)**: Karya seni yang melibatkan bunyi dan alunan nada.

10. **Komunikasi (Communication)**: Proses berbagi informasi atau pesan antara individu atau kelompok.

11. **Manajemen (Management)**: Praktik pengelolaan dan pengorganisasian sumber daya dalam bisnis atau organisasi.

12. **Marketing (Marketing)**: Kegiatan promosi dan pemasaran produk atau jasa.

13. **Globalisasi (Globalization)**: Proses penyebaran budaya, perdagangan, dan komunikasi di seluruh dunia.

14. **Edukasi (Education)**: Proses pembelajaran dan pendidikan.

15. **Demokrasi (Democracy)**: Sistem pemerintahan di mana kekuasaan dipegang oleh rakyat.

16. **Karier (Career)**: Perjalanan profesional seseorang dalam dunia kerja.

17. **Inovasi (Innovation)**: Pengembangan produk atau ide yang baru dan lebih efisien.

18. **Desain (Design)**: Proses perancangan atau rancang bangun.

19. **Kualitas (Quality)**: Tingkat keunggulan atau mutu suatu produk atau layanan.

20. **Teknologi (Technology)**: Penggunaan ilmu pengetahuan untuk menciptakan alat, mesin, dan perangkat elektronik.

Itu hanyalah sebagian kecil dari kata-kata serapan dari bahasa Inggris ke dalam bahasa Indonesia. Penggunaan kata-kata serapan ini mencerminkan pengaruh budaya, teknologi, dan globalisasi dalam perkembangan bahasa Indonesia.

Answer:

There's no certain question which can appear in the interview test because all of the questions in interview test is asked randomly depending on the interviewer and the term and condition. Before, when I was going to have an interview test for senior high school, I asked my relatives and friends who had experienced it before. There were some similar questions and the rest was so different. So, here are some common questions that might be asked in interview question according to my research at that time.
1. Introducing yourself
2. The reason why you choose that school to continue your study
3. Describing something
4. Your opinion or point of view on something.

In addition, here are my senior high school interview questions:
1. Could you please tell me about yourself?
2. Could you tell me about your family?
3. Why do you choose our school to continue your study?
4. How will you help this school develop better?
5. What do you think about high schooler drug abuse?
6. Can you describe how is your junior high school?

I hope the answer is clear enough and I hope it can help you to face the interview test with more confidence.

27. contoh soal tek bahasa ingris wawancara


Sinta : Excuse me, Sir. May I come in?
Alfon : Yes. Please, come in and have a seat.
Sinta : Thank you, Sir.
Alfon : So, your name is Sinta, right?
Sinta : Yes, that is right, Sir.
Alfon : Very well, my name is Alfon. I am the head of the human resource department of this hotel company.
Sinta : Nice to meet you, Sir.
Alfon : How are you this morning, Sinta?
Sinta : I’m perfectly fine, Sir. Thank you, and how about you?
Alfon : I’m fine too. Thank you. Did you get caught under the rain on your way coming here, Sinta? It rained this morning.
Sinta : it was just a cloudy day in my place, Sir. It didn’t rain when I left my house to come here. So, the rain must have stopped before I arrived here.
Alfon : I see, I’m happy for you.
Sinta : Thank you, Sir.
Alfon : So, do you know what we are going to do, Sinta?
Sinta : Yes, Sir. I was emailed and called by phone in order to have a job interview session from this company three days ago. So, now here I’m going to be having an interview job session with you.
Alfon : Very well. Can you tell me about your self, Sinta?
Sinta : My name is Sinta Rahmawati. I’m 23 years old. I graduated from one of State Universities in Bandung. I took hotel-management as my major. I love travelling and singing. Travelling and singing both improve my health mentally and physically.
Alfon : You applied to be our Junior Hotel Manager. Do you have any experience of this job?
Sinta : No, Sir. I am a fresh graduate. This is my first time to apply a full time job.
Alfon : What makes you decide to apply for Junior Hotel Manager position?
Sinta : I think Junior Hotel Manager is an appropriate job position for me. I find it attractive and interesting as well. Due to the hotel-management major I took, I’m sure that my skills and talents will be conducive to this company’s success. I can also see a bright future of my career by joining this company.
Alfon : Very well. So, what is your strength and weakness point?
Sinta : My strength is my cheerfulness, my carefulness, my strong will to learn new things, and my skill to work under pressures and as a team. My weakness is my phobia of dark.
Alfon : Well, it was a great time to have a little talk with you, Sinta. We will announce the result of your interview in a week. Thank you for coming here.
Sinta : Yes. Thank you, Sir.
