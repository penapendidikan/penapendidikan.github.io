2. tolong ya soal materi past tense​


Susunlah kata-kata ini agar menjadi benar. Kemudian tulis dalam bentuk kalimat PAST TENSE.

Pembahasan :
Rumus simple PAST nominal adalah
(+) subject + To be (was/were) + complement
(-) subject + To be (was/were) + not + complement
(?) To be (was/were) + subject + complement

Catatan !!!
* To be "was" digunakan apabila subject nya I/she/he/it/orang ketiga tunggal.
* To be "were" digunakan apabila subject nya you/they/we/plural subject.

Jadi, jawabannya adalah :
1). Andra WAS the smartest student in School.
=> Andra adalah murid paling pintar di sekolah.

2). I WAS very sleepy this morning.
=> Saya sangat mengantuk pagi ini.

3). We WERE sorry for him.
=> Kami minta maaf untuknya.

4). She WAS a nice neighbour.
=> Dia tetangga yang baik.

5). The house WAS damage because of the flood.
=> Rumah itu rusak karena banjir.

6). Mother WAS not in Bandung yesterday.
=> Ibu tidak berada di bandung kemarin.

7). The concert WAS fantastic.
=> Konser itu luar biasa.

8). Where WERE you last night?
=> Dimana kamu kemarin malam?

9). John WAS not with Tammy at the movies.
=> John tidak bersama Tammy di bioskop.

10). WAS he late yesterday?
=> Apakah dia terlambat kemarin?

8. carilah materi tentang simple past tense​


Jawaban:

Simple present tense merupakan tenses yang digunakan untuk membicarakan sesuatu yang umum, sesuatu yang selalu terjadi berulang-ulang, atau suatu kebenaran umum. Simple present tense adalah tenses yang paling sering digunakan sehari-hari.

Untuk mengenali bahwa sebuah kalimat merupakan simple present tense, ada beberapa cara yang bisa dilakukan, salah satunya adalah kalimat tersebut menggunakan kata kerja bentuk dasar (Verb 1). Simple present tense terbagi menjadi dua pola kalimat, yaitu verbal simple present dan nominal simple present tense.

Jawaban:

Simple past tense digunakan untuk membicarakan suatu kejadian yang terjadi di masa lampau dan berakhir pula di masa lampau dalam waktu yang spesifik. Kalimat dalam simple past tense ditandai dengan kata kerja kedua (verb2) yang menunjukkan waktu lampau.

Pola kalimat simple past tense terbagi menjadi 2 rumus, yaitu verbal simple past tense dan nominal simple past tense.

1. Verbal Simple Past

(+)

Subject + Verb2 + Object

(-)

Subject + did + not + verb1

(?)

Did + Subject + Verb1

Pada simple past tense, kata kerja kedua (V2) hanya digunakan pada kalimat positif. Jika bentuk kalimatnya negatif dan pertanyaan, maka akan kembali ke kata kerja dasar pertama dengan menambahkan did sebelum kata kerja.

Contoh kalimat verbal simple past tense:

-My mom and I went to the zoo yesterday

2. Nominal Simple Past

(+)

Subject + to be (was/were) + adjective/adverb

(-)

Subject +to be (was/were) + adjective/adverb

(?)

to be (was/were) + Subject + adjective/adverb

Was digunakan untuk subjek I, She, He, It

Were digunakan untuk subjek You, They, We

Contoh kalimat nominal simple past tense:

I was here last night

28. buatlah soal past tense + past perfect (5),past tense + past continous(5)


A. PAST TENSE VS PAST CONTINUOUS
1. My mother called me last night, when I was studying
2. The children were watching television, when their parents came home
3. You were making dinner, when my friends went home
4. When she was cleaning the windows, her husband called me
5. I was sleeping, when my brother woke me up

Note :
Gunakan PAST CONTINUOUS pada kalimat atau kejadian yang terjadi lebih dulu. Dalam bahasa Indonesia bisa diartikan "..... SEDANG MELAKUKAN".
Baik PAST TENSE dan PAST CONTINUOUS digunakan untuk kalimat yang sudah terjadi di masa lampau

B. PAST TENSE VS PAST PERFECT
1. When she was 21 years old, she had married
2. He had a luxury car, before he was 30 years old
3. They had had lunch, before they went to the library
4. Before we watched television, we had had done our homework
5. After they had gone to Paris, they went to England

Note :
Gunakan PAST PERFECT pada kalimat atau kejadian yang sudah selesai dikerjakan, dan dilanjutkan kejadian selanjutnya dimana kalimatnya menggunakan SIMPLE PAST. Dalam bahasa Indonesia, PAST PERFECT bisa diartikan ".... SUDAH MELAKUKAN".

30. Contoh cerita bahasa inggris yang didalam 1 cerita terdapat simple past tense, present perfect tense, past perfect tense


Haunted theatre
Last week, me and my family went to XXI in Kuningan city mall of Jakarta. When we arrived at the mall, we directly rushed to XXI cinema and bought the tickets. After we bought the tickets, we went to have lunch. We had our lunch in Ichiban sushi. We checked the time and it's our turn to watch so we decided to hurry and go to the cinema. When we arrived, the cinema was very deserted and quiet. Then we finally realized that there was no one. Not a single soul except the silent waiter, the ticket cashier and us. The atmosphere was really quiet and my mother was scared so we entered the the studio quickly. Inside the studio, there was no one again. I started to feel the chills and cringed. Not long later the lamps was turned off and the movie began. During the movie, I felt something or someone touched me. I was curious so I looked behind. I saw a shadow of a man without the person. I thought my minds was playing tricks with me so i just forget about the shadow and enjoyed the movie. After the movie finished, I asked my mother and father whether they had felt someone touched them. They said that they also felt the same. Suddenly, a nearby lamp explodes by itself! Me and my family was really shocked and ran away to the parking lot. We rushed home as fast as we can. At home, I did a research about the cinema and found out that there was a person called Mr. Jenovocic who died during the fire in the cinema. When I read the review, people said that his shadow and soul never left the cinema and always appear on 23rd May, the day he died. I also found out that 23rd May was the day when I and my family went there. I faced left and right to check if there are any shadows. Fortunately, there are no shadows. But, I forgot to face behind. 32. buatlah kesimpulan tentang materi simple past tense​


SIMPLE PAST TENSE

Simple Past Tense adalah kalimat tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang terjadi di masa lampau dan telah berakhir di masa lampau.Simple Past Tense ada yang verbal dan ada juga yang nominal.

THE FORMULA (RUMUS) :

● Verbal

S + Verb 2 + Object (Positive)S + did + not + Verb 1 (Negative)Did + S + Verb 1 ? (Interrogative)

● Nominal

S + was / were + non verb (Positive)S + was / were + not + non verb (Negative)Was / were + S + non verb ? (Interrogative)

EXAMPLES (CONTOH) :

~ VERBAL ~

● Positive

My sister studied English last night. I cleaned my room yesterday.

● Negative

My sister did not study English last night.I did not clean my room.

● Interrogative

Did my sister study English last night?Did I clean my room yesterday?

~ NOMINAL ~

● Positive

They were in Jakarta last week.She was so sad.

● Negative

They were not in Jakarta last week.She was not so sad.

● Interrogative

Were they in Jakarta last week?Was she so sad?

36. Contoh cerita menggunakan simple past tense past continous tense dan past perfect tense


Pembahasan:


Sebelum membuat cerita yang menggunakan simple past tense, past continuous tense, dan past perfect tense, kita sebaiknya mengetahui struktur kalimat yang menggunakan simple past tense, past continuous tense, dan past perfect tense terlebih dahulu. Berikut penjelasannya.



Simple past tense

Tenses ini digunakan untuk mengekspresikan suatu kejadian atau kegiatan yang dilakukan pada masa lampau.

Struktur kalimat:

(+) S + V2 + O

(-) S + didn't / did not + V1 + O

(?) Did + S + V1 + O + ?

Contoh kalimat: I watched the World Cup on TV last night.



Past continuous tense

Tenses ini digunakan menyatakan suatu kejadian atau kegiatan yang sedang berlangsung di masa lampau.

Struktur kalimat:

(+) S + was / were + V-ing + O

(-) S + wasn't / weren't + V-ing + O

(?) Was / were + S + V-ing + O + ?

Contoh kalimat: I was cooking dinner when you called me yesterday


Past perfect tense

Tenses ini digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian yang telah terjadi sebelum kejadian lain pada masa lampau.

Struktur kalimat:

(+) S + had + V3 + O

(-) S + had + not + V3 + O

(?) Had + S + V3 + O +?

Contoh kalimat: She had had breakfast before she went to school.


Berikut contoh cerita yang menggunakan simple past tense, past continuous tense, dan past perfect tense.


Last week was my birthday. My family members seemed like they forgot my birthday. They didn't say happy birthday or gave a birthday present for me. They just did their daily routine as usual like there was nothing special. At school, my friends also didn't remember my birthday. It made me so sad. I went home early after the class had finished. I went to my bedroom when i had arrived at home. I cried because i thought nobody remembered my birthday. Suddenly, my bedroom's door opened when i was crying. I saw my family and my friends brought a birthday cake for me. I wiped my tears when they were singing happy birthday. Apparently, they had secretly planned to trick me because they knew that my birthday was coming soon. it was an unforgettable birthday surprise for me.
